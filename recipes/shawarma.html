Shawarma Recipe

Ingredients and Recipe

Shawarma is marinated with various seasonings and spices such as cumin, turmeric, and paprika. It is made by stacking thinly sliced meat, typically lamb, beef, or chicken, on a large rotating skewer or cone.

  • Meat of choice
  • marinate of choice
  • Onions
  • tomatoes

Steps to follow

  1. Thinly slice pieces of desired meats
  2. Place the sliced meat in a bowl and marinate it overnight for best results
  3. Refrigerate your mixture
  4. Pile your meat on a skewer
  5. Roast your meat over medium low heat as you smear more of that marinade on it
  6. Cook to preferred stage
  7. Ready to eat
