Ingredients and Recipe

A classic waffle recipe includes basic ingredients you probably already have on hand, creating a perfectly crisp breakfast item.

  • All purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps to follow

  1. Mix 2 cups of flour, 1 tea spoon of salt and 2 table spoons of sugar in a bowl
  2. Add half a cup of milk and a quarter cup of water to the mixture
  3. Add 5mgs of melted butter and 1 egg and mix until the mixture is light
  4. Pour about a 2 table spoons of the mixture on a pre heated skillet at medium heat
  5. Cook till gliden brown on both sides
  6. Pour honey or a syrup of choice on your waffles
  7. serve when hot
