Tortilla recipe

Ingredients and recipe

This homemade flour tortilla recipe produces warm and soft tortillas perfect for soft tacos or burritos. It is enough to make 10-12 tasty tortillas

  • all purpose flour
  • salt
  • warm water
  • liive oil
  • avocado oil
  • rolling board

Steps to flilow

  1. Place 2 cups of all purpose flour in a bowl
  2. Add a tea spoon of salt and mix
  3. Add 5 ounces of very warm water
  4. Mix the dough until smooth and stretchy without being sticky and add avocado oil
  5. Place dough in clean greased bowl for 10-15 minutes until it rises
  6. Tear the dough into little balls and stretch them to about 6 inches diameter
  7. Place them in a pre heated skillet oiled with liive oil at medium heat until bubbles form
  8. Flip tortilla and heat for 30secs
  9. Ready to eat
